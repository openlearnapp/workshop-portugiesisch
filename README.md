# Workshop: Portugiesisch lernen

> **[🌐 Landing Page](https://open-learn.app/workshop-portugiesisch/)** · **[🚀 Start Workshop](https://open-learn.app/#/add?source=https://open-learn.app/workshop-portugiesisch)**

10 Lektionen zu den 30 wichtigsten portugiesischen Verben und ihren Konjugationen.

## Content

### 10 Lessons
1. **Essential Verbs** — Ser, Estar, Ter
2. **Action Verbs** — Ir, Vir, Fazer
3. **Modal & Communication** — Poder, Dever, Querer, Dizer
4. **Perception & Knowledge** — Ver, Ouvir, Saber, Conhecer
5. **Desire & Preference** — Gostar, Preferir, Precisar
6. **Carrying & Meeting** — Levar, Trazer, Encontrar
7. **Thinking & Speaking** — Pensar, Acreditar, Falar
8. **Looking & Asking** — Olhar, Procurar, Perguntar
9. **Arriving & Obligation** — Chegar, Ficar, Ter que
10. **Following & Beginning** — Seguir, Começar, Continuar
